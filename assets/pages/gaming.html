Love For Gaming

There are many e-games throughout the world and millions of people playing it. This has given the e-gaming platform to reach the next level. There are games like battle royal, sports , puzzles ,adventures etc. that has been growing in the modern world. Some of the games like DOTA 2 which is a multiplayer MMO RPG game has a prize pool of $40 million. Mind blowing, isn't it? These so called games are not as easy as it sounds, gamers take up lot of time practicing and trying to get better at these games.In the middle of all of this, We fell in love with the grind, the pressure and the amount of IQ that you have to use to win battles. Without us knowing, we fell in love with competitive online gaming.
