All about our club
Welcome to Zero Esports – the ultimate gaming team dominating the arenas of Dota 2 and Counter-Strike 2 (CS2). Founded by a group of passionate gamers, Zero Esports has rapidly become one of the fiercest competitors in the professional esports scene. We pride ourselves on teamwork, strategic innovation, and a relentless drive to win. Join us as we continue our journey toward esports greatness!.
At Zero Esports, gaming is more than just competition – it's a lifestyle. Our players embody the spirit of esports, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in each match they play. Whether it's the split-second decisions in Dota 2 or the precise shots in CS2, Zero Esports consistently delivers jaw-dropping performances that leave fans and opponents in awe.
